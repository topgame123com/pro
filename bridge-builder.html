Game Description

Bridge Builder Simulator is a physics-based puzzle game where you step into the shoes of a design engineer tasked with constructing sturdy bridges. Using limited materials, you must improvise, adapt, and balance logic with creativity to support passing vehicles. Across diverse locations and increasingly complex challenges, each structure you build tests your problem-solving skills and engineering finesse.
