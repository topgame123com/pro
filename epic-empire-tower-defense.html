Game Description

Epic Empire: Tower Defense is a thrilling strategy game where you command powerful heroes and towers to protect your kingdom from waves of evil forces. Embark on an epic adventure through vibrant worlds, from frozen kingdom, soaring magical kingdoms, dark underground tunnels, and enchanted florists. Face unique challenges worldwide as you battle to reclaim territories overrun by enemies. Take charge of diverse heroes such as Kamuii the mighty Ninja, Khalani the wizard, the glorious Phoenix, and other mysterious figures. Utilize archers, Infantry, magic towers, and stone golem to defend against waves of goblins, ogres, and more. Enhance your heroes with a vast arsenal of runes and power items, and unlock their true potential. Compete in global tournaments to showcase your tower defense prowess and earn big rewards. Get ready to defend your castle and kingdom from evil forces! Join the global community of players and demonstrate your mastery in Epic Empire - Tower Defense! Will you rise to the challenge and claim victory?
